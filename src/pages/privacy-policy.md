# Privacy Policy

I take privacy seriously. This page explains exactly what data this site
collects, how it's used, and what third-party services are involved. Nothing
more, nothing less.

## Cookies

This website uses **no cookies**. No tracking cookies, no session cookies, no
cookies of any kind.

## Analytics

This website has **no analytics**. I do not track page views, visitor counts,
or any other usage data.

## Contact form

If you submit the [contact form](/contact/), your name, email address, and
message are forwarded to me via the Telegram API. This information is used
solely to respond to your message and is not stored in any database, shared
with any third party, or used for any other purpose.

## Third-party services

This site makes use of a small number of third-party services:

- **YouTube** — the Music page embeds YouTube videos via YouTube's player. If
  you interact with a video, YouTube's standard privacy policy applies.
- **Lichess** — the About page fetches publicly available chess stats from the
  [Lichess API](https://lichess.org/api). No personal data is sent; it is a
  read-only request for public information.
- **unpkg.com** — two small scripts (`usal` and `dayjs`) are loaded from the
  [unpkg](https://unpkg.com) CDN. These scripts have no tracking capability and
  are verified with Subresource Integrity (SRI) hashes.

## Your rights

You are welcome to [contact me](/contact/) at any time with questions or
requests relating to your personal data.
